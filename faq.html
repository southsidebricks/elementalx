FAQ. Answered by Year Up TA's and instructors.

Hardware

  • What's the best certification to get into the IT field for hardware? The A+ certification is a great place to start. Cisco certifications are more difficult to obtain, but very marketable and recognized globally.
  • Should I buy a new computer or upgrade my own? As long as it is not too old, you can probably save money upgrading rather than buying an entirely new machine. Customization is great because you can optimize your computer to fit your needs.
  • What OS do most companies use? The answer is probably Windows, so it is important to familiarize yourself with it.

Software

  • What is the best certification for software? Starting with an MOS or MTA certification is a good way to prove your skills.
  • What software application should I know before entering the corporate world? Excel is widely used in finance, tech, and most other businesses, or at least some sort of software similar to it.
  • What is SQL? It is not a programming language, but rather a query language used in applications like MS Access. It has to do with data, databases, and sorting information.

Web Development

  • What's the difference between front-end and back-end programming? Front-end has to do with websites and the User Experience, and how people see/interact with sites. Back-end is what happens behind the scenes, gathering and storing data, managing systems, and staying on the server-side.
  • What languages should I focus on learning? It depends on what your end goal is. If you primarily want to work with website development, you should begin with HTML and CSS.
  • How can I get my website up and running? You will need a a web hosting service. Some, like GitHub, offer free web hosting.

Business Communication

  • What's the difference between a Cover Letter and Business Letter? The main purpose of the cover letter is to talk more about your resume, and explain your specific talents for the company you are applying to.
  • When should I send a follow up email? Ideally, you should send it within the next 2 days of meeting the person. Don't send in within the first 5 hours, so they remember you after the first day.
  • How marketable are soft skills? It's important to remember that good communication is vital in the corporate world. Hiring managers look for people that are skilled, but also good coworkers. Be someone that they wouldn't mind going out to lunch with.

Tracks

  • When will I know what track I'm in? At the beginning of Module 3. Your performance, attitude, and indicated interest will all be taken into account.
  • What if I get placed in a track I didn't choose? Not likely.
  • What about the web development track? There is no such thing. It is a branch of the Software track, and there are other "branches", but they fall under either Hardware or Software.
  • Can I do two tracks at once? On your own time, maybe, but officially, the answer is probably no.
